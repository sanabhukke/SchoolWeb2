School Library
Was built in 1980

The library is home to over 400 books. During library times, the kids to fun activities, read books, and check out books. Because of our school library, 1st graders have started reading huge chapter books!



School Auditorium
Was built in 1982

Here, the school choir performs, skits and drama plays are held here, and lastly, all the students gather here for school assemblies.
